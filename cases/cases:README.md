## Understanding the Logs

Each case is a "specimen" of decision-making. We move beyond "what was said" to analyze "what happened to the meaning."

### The SRCM Analysis
When reviewing these cases, we look at:
- **S (Speaker):** What was the implicit intent?
- **R (Receiver):** What internal model did the receiver apply?
- **C (Constraint):** What power, budget, or structural filter intervened?
- **M (Meaning):** What action was actually triggered?
